BandarBan-the most adventurous tour of us

September 2020 during Covid lockdown 12 wonderlusts started the journey to Bandarban and decided to completed the most dangerous track. Almost 80% time it was heavily raining, had to cross river more than 6 times, walked more than 12 hours everyday, night travel was more than 6 hours, completely dangerous hills, sometimes it took 2-4 hours to up and same down. Final destination Amikhum was the most dangerous one due to rain and hight angle. But enjoyed a lot mostly the beauty, adventure, swiming more than 2 hours with the tide, jumping to Nafakhum, 2nd height waterfall, and as always our own 12 wonderlusts and foods.
